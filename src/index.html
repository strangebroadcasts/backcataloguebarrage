The Rules

  • For each band/artist listed, name one of their songs, except their most popular.
  • You get 30 minutes to answer.
  • Capitalization, punctuation and parentheticals don't matter - if the song is named "C.Y.D.T.T. (Can You Dance To This?)", "cydtt" is a valid answer.
  • Tracks where MusicBrainz lists the band/artist as main author(s) (as of January 31st 2025) qualify.
